Scholarship Details

Application Procedure

+

Applicants must apply through the KAAD partner committee in their home country (or via the KAAD online system if no committee is available).

Initial application involves submitting academic records, CV, letter of motivation, and recommendation through the pre-selection process. Final application follows after nomination.

Application deadline is June 30, 2025.

Selection & Evaluation Criteria

+

Applicants are selected based on:

  • Academic excellence and prior performance
  • Alignment of study plan with home country development
  • Commitment to returning to home country
  • Strong moral character and social responsibility
  • Religious affiliation (preferably Catholic, but others may apply)

Final selection is made by KAAD in cooperation with church-related partner institutions.

Amount and Benefits

+

KAAD scholarship covers:

  • Monthly stipend: approx. €992 (Master’s), up to €1,300 (Ph.D.)
  • Full tuition coverage
  • German language course (if required)
  • Travel costs
  • Health insurance
  • Accommodation assistance (as needed)

Scholarship duration aligns with the academic program (typically 2 years for Master's).

Academic Requirements and Commitment

+

Scholarship holders must:

  • Be admitted to a German university for a full-time program
  • Submit academic progress reports regularly
  • Participate in KAAD seminars and networking activities
  • Commit to return and contribute to development in their home country

Failure to comply may result in termination of scholarship benefits.

How to Apply

1

Choose a Master's Program

Explore the available Master's programs at universities in Germany. Make sure the program aligns with your academic and career goals.

2

Submit Application Online

Submit your complete admission application through the DAAD scholarship portal. You must upload all required documents.

3

Prepare Required Documents

Ensure that you provide all necessary documents, including:

  • Certified academic transcripts
  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Motivation letter
  • Proof of language proficiency (English or German)
  • Passport copy
4

Automatic Scholarship Consideration

Once you submit your application, all eligible applicants will automatically be considered for the DAAD Master Scholarship. No separate application is needed.
