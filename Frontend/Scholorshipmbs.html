Scholarship Details

Application Procedure

+

To apply for the MBS Education Fund, students must complete the official online application form provided by Brain Capital.

Required documents include:

  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Bachelor's degree transcripts
  • Admission confirmation from Munich Business School
  • Valid passport or identity card

Once your application is reviewed and found suitable, you will be contacted for a personal interview to discuss the funding model.

Selection & Evaluation Criteria

+

Applicants are evaluated based on:

  • Academic background and performance
  • Overall profile and motivation
  • Admission to Munich Business School (Bachelor or Master program)

After a successful review, a funding model discussion is held to ensure transparency and mutual understanding.

Amount and Payment of the Scholarship

+

The MBS Education Fund covers 100% of your tuition fees upfront. The funding amount depends on each student’s individual financial need and program cost.

There is no fixed amount or monthly stipend. Instead, the fund provides a flexible financing model tailored to your situation.

Course Attendance and Academic Standing

+

Students funded by the MBS Education Fund must:

  • Remain enrolled in their selected Bachelor's or Master's program at Munich Business School
  • Maintain satisfactory academic progress
  • Comply with all institutional regulations and academic requirements

Failure to comply with academic or behavioral standards may impact your funding agreement.

How to Apply

1

Quick and Easy Application

Complete the online form and attach the required documents:
- CV
- Bachelor transcripts
- Admission confirmation from the university
- Identity card or passport

2

Profile Check

We’ll review your application to ensure you meet our funding criteria. Your academic background and overall profile are considered. If selected, we’ll invite you for a personal chat.

3

Funding Model Discussion

We will explain our education fund model in detail through a phone or video call. You’ll get a chance to ask questions before deciding to proceed with the funding offer.

4

Start Your Journey

Once you sign the contract, the education fund will cover your tuition. You can fully focus on your studies, and we’ll be there to support you if any financing questions arise.
