Scholarship Details

Application Procedure

+

To apply for UAM scholarships for international students, candidates must:

  • Apply and gain acceptance to an undergraduate or graduate program at the Autonomous University of Madrid.
  • Complete the scholarship application form available on the UAM official website.
  • Submit supporting documents such as academic transcripts, proof of financial need, and a personal statement.

Deadlines and specific procedures are published annually on UAM’s scholarship portal.

Selection & Evaluation Criteria

+

Scholarship applications are evaluated based on:

  • Academic excellence and performance in previous studies
  • Demonstrated financial need
  • Clarity and motivation in the personal statement
  • Overall application completeness and strength

Amount and Benefits of the Scholarship

+

UAM scholarships offer:

  • Full or partial tuition fee waivers
  • Financial assistance for living expenses
  • Opportunities for academic and research development
  • Professional networking and career support

Note: While accommodation is not included directly in the scholarship, UAM offers housing support services to help international students find suitable living arrangements.

Course Attendance and Academic Standing

+

Recipients must:

  • Remain enrolled full-time in their respective degree program
  • Maintain satisfactory academic progress throughout the academic year
  • Follow all university regulations and actively engage in academic life

Failure to meet academic or conduct standards may result in revocation of the scholarship.

How to Apply

1

Choose a Program

Select a Bachelor’s or Master’s degree program offered at the Autonomous University of Madrid (UAM) from the official academic catalog.

2

Apply for Admission

Complete the application for your chosen program via the UAM online admission platform. Ensure you meet the program-specific requirements.

3

Prepare Scholarship Documents

Gather necessary documents including academic transcripts, proof of financial need, a personal statement, and recommendation letters (if required).

4

Submit Scholarship Application

Apply for the scholarship via the UAM scholarships portal, indicating your program and uploading all required documentation. Follow deadlines posted on the official site.
