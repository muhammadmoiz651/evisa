Description

Hotel Wunderlocke Munich is a stylish and modern aparthotel located in the heart of Munich’s Sendling district. Designed to combine the comfort of home with the convenience of a hotel, it offers thoughtfully curated studio apartments ideal for students, business travelers, and long-stay guests.

Each unit is equipped with a private kitchenette, ensuite bathroom, high-speed Wi-Fi, and cozy furnishings. The hotel also features an on-site fitness studio, co-working space, sauna, rooftop terrace, and an outdoor pool — making it a vibrant space to live, work, and relax.

Located near public transport links, supermarkets, and green spaces, Wunderlocke provides excellent connectivity to central Munich and major universities. Its creative and community-focused atmosphere makes it an ideal choice for international students and young professionals seeking both functionality and style.
