Scholarship Details

Application Procedure

+

To apply for MEFP Grants at the Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC), students must:

  • Be enrolled or have applied to an official undergraduate or master's degree at UPC.
  • Apply through the official MEFP scholarship portal (typically hosted on the Spanish Ministry of Education's website).
  • Provide all required documents such as academic transcripts, proof of income, and bank details.

The application typically opens in March and must be completed by May each academic year.

Selection & Evaluation Criteria

+

Applications are evaluated based on the following criteria:

  • Enrollment in a qualifying UPC program
  • Academic performance in the previous year or entrance qualifications
  • Demonstrated financial need based on family income

Additional points may be awarded for exceptional academic achievements or challenging personal circumstances.

Amount and Benefits of the Scholarship

+

The MEFP Grants provide:

  • Partial or full tuition fee coverage
  • Annual living allowance (amount varies based on income and location)
  • Additional support for academic excellence or housing

The final amount is determined after evaluation by the Ministry of Education and can vary year to year.

Course Attendance and Academic Standing

+

To retain the scholarship, recipients must:

  • Remain enrolled full-time at UPC
  • Pass a minimum percentage of enrolled credits
  • Maintain good academic standing throughout the scholarship period

Failure to comply may lead to cancellation and refund of the scholarship amount.

How to Apply

1

Select a Degree Program

Choose a Bachelor's or Master's program from the official offerings of the Polytechnic University of Catalonia (UPC). Ensure it is an officially recognized program eligible for MEFP grants.

2

Apply for Admission to UPC

Submit your application through UPC's official admissions portal. Make sure to meet the academic and language requirements of your selected program.

3

Prepare MEFP Scholarship Documents

Gather documents including previous academic records, proof of family income, identification documents, and other forms required by the Spanish Ministry of Education.

4

Apply for the MEFP Grant

Submit your scholarship application through the MEFP online platform (accessible via the Ministry of Education website). Monitor deadlines carefully and ensure all documents are uploaded correctly.
