Scholarship Details

Application Procedure

+

Applicants must apply through the ENS International Selection program. The application process includes submitting academic transcripts, a CV, a motivation letter, and letters of recommendation. Specific requirements may vary by department.

Applications are typically open annually, with deadlines and procedures outlined on the official ENS website.

Selection & Evaluation Criteria

+

Selection is based on:

  • Academic excellence and achievements
  • Strength of the motivation letter
  • Quality of letters of recommendation
  • Performance in interviews or examinations, if applicable

Proficiency in French or English is required, depending on the program.

Amount and Benefits of the Scholarship

+

Successful candidates receive:

  • A monthly stipend of approximately €1,000 for three years
  • Accommodation on the ENS campus
  • Access to ENS resources and facilities

These benefits support students throughout their studies at ENS.

Course Attendance and Academic Standing

+

Scholarship recipients are expected to:

  • Maintain full-time enrollment in their chosen program
  • Achieve satisfactory academic progress each year
  • Comply with ENS's academic and conduct policies

Failure to meet these standards may result in the revocation of the scholarship.

How to Apply

1

Check Eligibility

Ensure you meet the eligibility criteria: international student (outside France), under 26 years old, and not having resided in France for more than 10 months during the academic year of application.

2

Select an Academic Track

Choose from eligible academic disciplines (Sciences or Arts & Humanities) offered through the ENS International Selection.

3

Submit Application Online

Complete and submit the online application through the ENS official portal. Required documents include transcripts, CV, motivation letter, and recommendation letters.

4

Await Evaluation

Your application will be reviewed by ENS academic committees. Shortlisted candidates may be invited for an interview or written examination.
