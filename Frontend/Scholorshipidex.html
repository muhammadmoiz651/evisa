Scholarship Details

Application Procedure

+

The application process includes submitting an online application form, academic transcripts, CV, recommendation letters, and a statement of purpose. All documents must be submitted in English or French.

Applicants must first apply to their desired Master's program at Paris-Saclay University before applying for this scholarship.

Programmes / Fields

+

The scholarship is available for all Master's programs offered at Paris-Saclay University, including but not limited to:

  • Engineering Sciences
  • Mathematics and Computer Science
  • Physics
  • Life Sciences and Health
  • Humanities and Social Sciences
  • Law, Economics, and Management

Selection of Laureates & Evaluation Criteria

+

The selection committee evaluates applications based on:

  • Academic excellence
  • Motivation and project coherence
  • Language proficiency (English or French)
  • Recommendations from previous institutions
  • Geographic diversity

Priority may be given to students from partner institutions and underrepresented regions.

Amount and Payment of the Scholarship

+

The IDEX International Master's Scholarship provides:

  • 10,000 Euros per year (for 10 months, from September to June)
  • Up to 1,000 Euros extra for travel and visa support depending on the student's country of origin

Payments are made monthly directly to the student's French bank account. Students must open a bank account upon arrival in France.

Course Attendance and Academic Mobility

+

Scholarship recipients must:

  • Maintain regular attendance in all courses
  • Achieve satisfactory academic progress
  • Notify the scholarship office of any intended absences

Academic mobility opportunities are available within the program, allowing students to spend a semester at partner institutions while maintaining their scholarship status.
