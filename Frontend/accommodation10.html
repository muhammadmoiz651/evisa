Description

The FIZZ München is a modern and student-friendly accommodation located in the heart of Munich. It offers fully furnished private apartments designed for comfort, convenience, and community living — perfect for international students, interns, and young professionals.

Each apartment includes a private bathroom, kitchenette, comfortable bed, workspace, and high-speed internet. The building features extensive shared facilities such as study rooms, community kitchens, lounges, a rooftop terrace, laundry rooms, and bike storage — creating an environment that supports both academic focus and social interaction.

Situated close to public transport, universities, supermarkets, and recreational areas, The FIZZ provides easy access to everything Munich has to offer. With flexible booking types, all-in rent pricing, and vibrant community events, it’s more than just a place to live — it’s a lifestyle designed for success and connection.
