Eiffel Excellence Scholarship

Supporting exceptional international students in their academic journey at Panthéon-Sorbonne University

0 Days
0 Hours
0 Minutes
0 Seconds

Application Deadline: October 28, 2025

Scholarship Overview

Masters Program

€1,181/month

PhD Program

€1,700/month

Benefits Include

  • Travel Assistance

    One round-trip international flight between your home country and France

  • Health Coverage

    Comprehensive health insurance for the duration of your studies.

  • Housing Support

    Assistance in finding accommodation in France through Campus France.

  • Cultural Activities

    Access to French cultural events and student gatherings.

Eligibility Criteria

  • Non-French nationality (priority to students residing outside France).
  • Age under 25 for master's applicants, under 30 for PhD applicants.
  • Excellent academic background and leadership potential.
  • English or French language proficiency as required by the program.

How to Apply

Applications for the Eiffel Excellence Scholarship must be submitted through Panthéon-Sorbonne University. Candidates cannot apply directly — they must be nominated by the university.
