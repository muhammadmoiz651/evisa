Description

Studentendorf Adlershof Berlin offers a vibrant and community-oriented living environment, specially designed for students. Located in the Adlershof district, it is within walking distance of Humboldt University’s Adlershof Campus, making it an ideal choice for university students.

The residence features well-furnished private rooms, modern shared kitchens, and social common areas that promote interaction among students. With convenient access to public transport, grocery stores, and cafés, Studentendorf Adlershof provides both comfort and connectivity for academic and everyday life.
