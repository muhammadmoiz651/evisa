Scholarship Details

Application Procedure

+

No separate application is required. All applicants to eligible Master's programs at Macromedia University of Applied Sciences are automatically considered for the Excellence Scholarship.

Ensure your admission application, including academic transcripts, CV, motivation letter, and proof of English proficiency, is submitted before the admission deadline.

Selection & Evaluation Criteria

+

Scholarship recipients are selected based on:

  • Outstanding academic performance (GPA between 1.0 and 1.5 or equivalent)
  • Evidence of motivation and personal commitment
  • Strength of supporting documents submitted with the application

International and local students are eligible. Applicants must be 30 years of age or younger.

Amount and Payment of the Scholarship

+

The Excellence Scholarship offers:

  • Partial tuition fee coverage ranging from 10% to 50%

The award is applied directly to the tuition invoice; no cash payments are provided.

Course Attendance and Academic Standing

+

Scholarship holders are expected to:

  • Maintain enrollment in a full-time Master's program at Macromedia University
  • Maintain good academic standing and progress during the study period
  • Follow university attendance and academic policies strictly

Failure to meet performance expectations may result in withdrawal of the scholarship.

How to Apply

1

Choose a Master's Program

Explore eligible Master’s programs at Macromedia University of Applied Sciences and select the one aligned with your academic and career goals.

2

Apply Online

Submit your full admission application through the official online portal. Ensure your documents are complete and meet university requirements.

3

Prepare Required Documents

Include your academic transcripts, CV, letter of motivation, proof of English language proficiency, and copy of your passport or ID.

4

Automatic Scholarship Consideration

Once your admission application is submitted, you will automatically be considered for the Excellence Scholarship based on merit. No separate form is needed.
