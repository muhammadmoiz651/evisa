Document Requirements

Visa Application Form

Completed and signed national visa application form

Photograph

Recent passport-size color photo with a light background

Valid Passport

Valid for the entire stay, not older than 10 years, with two blank pages

Proof of Admission

Acceptance letter from a recognized Spanish educational institution

Financial Means

Proof of sufficient funds (€600–€900/month) to cover your stay

Health Insurance

Insurance from a Spanish-authorized company covering entire stay

Police Clearance

Criminal record certificate from past 5 years, legalized & translated

Medical Certificate

Stating you do not suffer from diseases listed in International Health Regulations

Proof of Residence

Proof that you reside or study in the Spanish Embassy's jurisdiction

Guardian Documents

If a minor, submit parent's ID and proof of relationship (originals + copies)

Parental Authorization

Notarized permission naming the guardian in Spain if the applicant is a minor

Visa Fee

Payment of visa fee (typically €80); may vary by nationality

Spain Student Visa Application Timeline

6+ Months Before

Research Spanish universities and programs; prepare necessary documents.

4–5 Months Before

Apply to selected universities; gather required documents for visa application.

3 Months Before

Receive acceptance letter; schedule visa appointment with BLS Spain Visa Center.

2 Months Before

Submit visa application; attend interview if required; await processing (typically 4–8 weeks).

1 Month Before

Receive visa; arrange accommodation and travel plans; prepare for departure.

After Arrival

Apply for Foreigner Identity Card (TIE) within 30 days at the local Foreigners' Office or Police Station.
