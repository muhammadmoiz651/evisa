Scholarship Details

Application Procedure

+

Applicants must submit an online application through the University of Barcelona portal. Required documents include academic transcripts, a portfolio (for arts-related fields), a motivation letter, and proof of language proficiency in Spanish or English.

It is recommended to apply early, as scholarship funds are limited and awarded on a rolling basis.

Selection & Evaluation Criteria

+

Selection is based on:

  • Artistic and academic merit
  • Strength of the portfolio
  • Motivation and alignment with chosen field
  • Language skills (Spanish or English)
  • Geographic and socio-economic background

Amount and Payment of the Scholarship

+

The University of Barcelona offers:

  • €8,000 per academic year
  • Additional €1,000 for travel and visa support for international students (case-by-case)

Funds are disbursed in two installments: 50% upon enrollment and 50% after the first semester based on academic progress.

Course Attendance and Academic Mobility

+

Recipients are expected to:

  • Maintain regular attendance in all courses
  • Actively participate in exhibitions and academic projects
  • Maintain good academic standing

Exchange opportunities are available within the Erasmus+ network.

How to Apply

1

Apply to University of Barcelona

Submit your application to your chosen Bachelor's or Master's program through the University of Barcelona's official portal.

2

Fill Scholarship Application

After university application, complete the scholarship form available on the Apply link.

3

Prepare & Upload Documents

Upload required documents including academic transcripts, portfolio (for art programs), CV, and a motivation letter.

4

Wait for Evaluation

Your application will be reviewed based on academic/artistic merit, motivation, and eligibility criteria.
