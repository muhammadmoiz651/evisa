Frequently Asked Questions

How long does the visa process take?

Processing times vary by country: USA (2-4 weeks), UK (3 weeks), Canada (4-8 weeks), Australia (4-6 weeks), Germany (4-6 weeks), France (2-3 weeks).

What are the language requirements?

Most countries require IELTS (6.0-6.5) or TOEFL (80-90). Germany may require TestDaF, and France may require DELF B2.

Can I work while studying?

Work hours vary by country: USA (20hrs/week on-campus), UK (20hrs/week), Canada (20hrs/week), Australia (40hrs/fortnight), Germany (120 full/240 half days/year).
