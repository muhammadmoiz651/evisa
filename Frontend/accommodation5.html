Description

Studelites Le Capitole offers contemporary student accommodation in the vibrant Latin Quarter of Paris, just a short walk from the prestigious École Normale Supérieure. This modern residence provides an ideal living space for students attending nearby universities in the 5th arrondissement.

The residence features fully-equipped private studios with modern furnishings, kitchenettes, and study areas. Residents benefit from excellent amenities including high-speed WiFi, laundry facilities, and 24/7 security. The prime location puts students within easy reach of academic institutions, cultural landmarks, and the lively atmosphere of the Left Bank.
