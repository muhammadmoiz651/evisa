Scholarship Details

Application Procedure

+

Applicants must apply online via the DAAD scholarship portal. The application includes submitting academic and personal documents.

All documents must be uploaded before the deadline: August 31, 2024. Applications submitted after the deadline will not be considered.

Selection & Evaluation Criteria

+

Candidates are evaluated based on:

  • Academic excellence and past performance
  • Well-structured and convincing study proposal
  • Strong motivation and clearly defined goals
  • Relevance of the chosen program to future career plans

The selection takes place in January 2025 and results are announced via the DAAD portal.

Amount and Benefits

+

The DAAD Master Scholarship covers:

  • Monthly stipend (approx. €934 for graduates)
  • Travel allowance (for international travel)
  • Health, accident, and personal liability insurance
  • Optional: Rent subsidy and allowance for accompanying family members

Funding begins in October 2025.

Academic Requirements and Commitment

+

Scholarship holders must:

  • Be enrolled in a full-time Master's program in Germany
  • Submit regular progress reports to DAAD
  • Maintain satisfactory academic performance during the scholarship period

Failure to meet academic requirements may result in withdrawal of funding.

How to Apply

1

Choose a Master's Program

Explore the available Master's programs at universities in Germany. Make sure the program aligns with your academic and career goals.

2

Submit Application Online

Submit your complete admission application through the DAAD scholarship portal. You must upload all required documents.

3

Prepare Required Documents

Ensure that you provide all necessary documents, including:

  • Certified academic transcripts
  • Curriculum Vitae (CV)
  • Motivation letter
  • Proof of language proficiency (English or German)
  • Passport copy
4

Automatic Scholarship Consideration

Once you submit your application, all eligible applicants will automatically be considered for the DAAD Master Scholarship. No separate application is needed.
